Projects

CubeHash FPGA Implementation

🏆 Open HW Finalist 2019

B.Sc. final project — a full hardware/software co-integration of CubeHash16/32-256, a cryptographic hash function submitted to the NIST SHA-3 competition. Implements the algorithm in Verilog on a Basys 3 (Artix-7) FPGA, communicating with a Raspberry Pi Zero for I/O. Achieved ~181 MHz clock frequency with minimal FPGA utilization.

PCB Business Card

A custom-designed PCB shaped as a business card, built entirely from discrete logic gates. Uses an NE555 timer generating a 1 Hz clock driving a 4-bit counter (D flip-flops), with combinational logic to display a phone number on 7-segment LEDs. Includes an integrated QR code with contact information. Powered by a CR2032 coin cell.
